Youru Li (李有儒)

Youru Li is a Professor with the College of Computer Science, Beijing University of Technology, Beijing, China. He has received the Ph.D. and M.E. degrees at the Department of Computer Science, Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU), Beijing, China in Jun. 2025 and Jun. 2020, respectively. From Jun. 2020 to Aug. 2021, he worked for Ant Financial, Alibaba, as a machine learning algorithm engineer. His current research interests include sequence data mining, knowledge graph and their applications on Health Informatic, RecSys and FinTech. He has published over 30 research papers in refereed conferences and journals (e.g., TPAMI, TKDE, TKDD, TORS, KDD, AAAI, MICCAI). He has received the Chinese Institute of Electronics (CIE) Excellent Master Dissertation Award in 2020 and the Second Prize for Technological Progress of Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAAI) WU Wenjun AI Science and Technology Award in 2023. He has been supported by the Inaugural Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CAST, Doctoral Student Special Plan. (Supported by the Chinese Institute of Electronics (CIE)) .
