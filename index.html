Youru Li (李有儒)

Professor, College of Computer Science

Youru Li is a Professor with the College of Computer Science, Beijing University of Technology, Beijing, China and also a Visiting Fellow at MIIT Key Laboratory of Data Intelligence and Management, Beihang University, Beijing, China. He has received the Ph.D. and M.E. degrees at the Department of Computer Science, Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU), Beijing, China.

His current research interests include sequence data mining, knowledge graph and their applications on Health Informatic, RecSys and FinTech. He has published over 30 research papers in refereed conferences and journals.
